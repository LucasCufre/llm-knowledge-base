## Stakeholder Communication Plan

### Regular Updates

| Stakeholder Group | Frequency | Method | Content | Owner |
|------------------|-----------|--------|---------|-------|
| Executive Sponsors | Monthly | Email summary | High-level status, risks, decisions needed | PM |
| Business Stakeholders | Bi-weekly | Status meeting | Progress, blockers, upcoming work | PM |
| Technical Stakeholders | Weekly | Stand-up/Slack | Technical progress, integration points | Tech Lead |
| End Users | Per milestone | Survey/Beta | Feature preview, feedback collection | Product Owner |
| Supporting Teams | As needed | Email/Meeting | Specific reviews and approvals | PM |

### Escalation Paths

| Issue Type | First Contact | Escalation Level 1 | Escalation Level 2 |
|-----------|---------------|-------------------|-------------------|
| Technical blocker | Tech Lead | Engineering Manager | CTO |
| Scope question | Product Owner | Project Sponsor | Executive Sponsor |
| Resource constraint | Project Manager | Department Head | Project Sponsor |
| External dependency | Project Manager | Partner Manager | Executive Sponsor |
